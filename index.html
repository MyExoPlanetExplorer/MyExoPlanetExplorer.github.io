Our Project

My friend and I are working on a project in which we have to design, build, and test a robot
using the LEGO Mindstorms EV3 kit. Our robot must autonomously traverse mazes
that simulate the unpredictable nature of a planet, such as the canyons of Mars. In other words, we must
design mazes and along robot that can successfully traverse it.
